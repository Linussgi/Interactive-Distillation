Binary Distillation

About

This page provides a basic visulisation of a binary distillation equilibrium graph.

You can manipulate the feed condition, reflux ratio and desired distillate purity of the lighter component using the sliders to see how the graph responds.

  • The two black lines represent the equilibrium data and the 45-line
  • The blue dashed lines represent the top and bottom operating lines of the column
  • The red line represents the q-line which is determined by the feed condition

For the purpose of this page, the bottom operating line remains fixed and will not change with any of the maipulated variables.
